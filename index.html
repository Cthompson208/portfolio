About Me

Introduction

Hi there! My name is Cheyenne and I'm a 24 year old beginner web developer! I'm from Post Falls, Idaho, and I started learning to code about a year and a half ago. Currently I am still taking courses online with Codecademy.com. So far, I have taken courses in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, learning how to build accessible and responsive websites. While I have been focused soley on front-end development so far, once I am confident in my front-end skills I plan to shift my focus to learning more about the back-end of web development. My goal is become a well versed full-stack engineer!

Inspiration

Fun Fact: I was inspired to get into programming because of the show Criminal Minds, a show about an FBI team catching serial killers! How on earth does that have anything to do with computers you ask? Well my favorite character on the show, Penelope Garcia, was the teams technical anaylst, and she was a badass. Ms. Garcia could do anything on a computer and was arguably the most important member of the BAU! Since I grew up watching this show with my mom I have very fond memories of it, and the desire to become as smart cool as Garcia has stuck with me through my life!
